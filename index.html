How it works

Three steps.
One real curry.

1
Melt the base
Drop the Currybomb into a pan with a little water. Medium heat, 3–4 minutes. Watch it come alive.
2
Add your choice
Paneer. Chicken. Chickpeas. Vegetables. Whatever's in your fridge. The base works with everything.
3
Simmer and serve
10–12 minutes. Season to taste. Plate it. It tastes like you spent all afternoon on it.
