How it works

Three steps.
One real curry.

The hardest part is already done.

1
Melt the base
Drop the Currybomb into a pan with a little water. Medium heat, 3–4 minutes.
2
Add your choice
Paneer, chicken, vegetables — whatever's in the fridge. The base works with everything.
3
Simmer and serve
10–12 minutes. Plate it. Tastes like you spent all afternoon on it.
